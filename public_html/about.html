What AGROCAM Entails

Our Vision and Mission

AGROCAM COMPANY LTD was founded in 2012 after an observation that Cameroon’s agriculture lags because of an abject lack in marketing and distribution infrastructure. Marketing and Sales cannot happen without the exchange of information, yet it is still difficult to access relevant authoritative content on agriculture in Cameroon. It is this finding that led the founding members to create an agency with theinable supply chains with multiple smallholder farmers..

Historical Review

AGROCAM COMPANY LTD was founded in 2012 as a Common Initiative Group (CIG) with headquarter in Bamenda. AGROCAM was later upgraded to a Cooperative Society Limited with Board of Directors in 2013. AGROCAM currently employs 8 and networks with a pool of experts and consultants from the fields of Agriculture, Food Processing, Marketing, Supply Chain Management, Project Management, Finance, ICT and Graphics Design. AGROCAM COMPANY LTD has direct access to nearly 2,000 farmers in Fako Division through their farmer cooperative body and can access over 8,000 farmers in the South West Region indirectly through agricultural extension workers who are part of our partner network. We share an office space with HIPDET polytechnic for higher education Mile 4 Bamenda Cameroon and another office operating at AKWA Douala Cameroon.
